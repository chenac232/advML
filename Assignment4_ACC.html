0. Data¶

I'm interested in seeing whether I can predict bank holdings of agency MBS using some financial markets data related to underlying interest rates.

Data is pulled from FRED (link here) and market data on treasury yields are pulled from WSJ.

Below are data I will be using:

  1. H8 bank holdings data of agency MBS (FRED) - this will be our target variable The rest are features that are known to have some relationship with bank holdings of MBS:
  2. H8 bank loans (FRED)
  3. H.4.1 Agency MBS holdings of SOMA portfolio (FRED)
  4. Fed funds rate (FRED)
  5. 5-year treasury yields (WSJ)
  6. 10-year treasury yields (WSJ)

Overall, the neural network model accuracy was pretty weak for this dataset for all three models tested.
